About me

I'm Denzel and I'm a Frontend Developer

I’ve been in IT Support since I was a child. My family was aware of my passion for technology so I was the one who hooked up their systems, fixed their Wi-Fi, helped them use their iPads, and would troubleshoot whatever problem cropped up. I didn’t mind because I love doing anything tech-related. As I studied the subject academically, I grew to love the idea of being a Front-end Developer. I love the process of taking the code and turning it into a masterpiece that can lead to better end-use experiences. I’m currently pursuing my goal of getting so familiar with the process that I don’t even have to think too much about it as my fingers glide over the keyboard. Ultimately though, I want to be an exceptional Full Stack Developer and, hopefully, start my own business. I plan to pursue Back-end Development once I’ve mastered the Front-end side of things. I believe I can achieve success in this field because not only do I love developing applications, I also still enjoy troubleshooting issues..
